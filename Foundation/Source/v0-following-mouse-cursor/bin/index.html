Hockey demo (NOTICE: blue team is 100% AI controlled)

Use mouse cursor to guide/aim your athlete. Click to hit the puck. Some cheating keys:

  • C: select another athlete to control;
  • R: place puck at the center of the rink;
  • G: organize the match (athletes take places);

This is a WIP and I've focused on the AI, so it's not that easy to play as a human.
The best part is to keep the mouse cursor at the center and watch the AI play :D.
